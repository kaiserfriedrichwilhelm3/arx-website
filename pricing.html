FAQ

Common questions.

What's included in the setup fee? +
The one-time setup fee covers agent training on your business/practice, integration configuration (CRM, calendar, EHR), testing and QA, and your team onboarding session. Once set up, the agent runs on the monthly subscription with no additional configuration costs for standard use cases.
How is AIVIS Medical different from the Business plans? +
AIVIS is purpose-built for clinical environments. It includes HIPAA-compliant infrastructure, a formal BAA, EHR write-back, deterministic triage state logic, hard-coded emergency escalation, and SOAP note automation. Business plans are designed for general SMB use cases and do not include clinical-grade compliance or medical integrations.
What happens if I exceed the included minutes? +
General Business plans: overage is billed at $0.25/min. AIVIS Elite includes 10,000 minutes — additional clinical minutes are $0.30/min. We'll notify you before you hit your limit and can upgrade your plan automatically if desired.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled with 30 days notice. Setup fees are non-refundable. AIVIS Medical plans include a 90-day minimum engagement due to the complexity of EHR integration and BAA requirements.
How long does deployment take? +
ARX Business plans: 24–48 hours for standard configurations. AIVIS Medical: 5–14 business days depending on EHR system and BAA execution. Enterprise engagements are scoped individually and typically run 4–8 weeks for full deployment.
