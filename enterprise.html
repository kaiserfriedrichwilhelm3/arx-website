The Process

How an Enterprise
engagement begins.

01
Technical Evaluation Request
Submit an application. We review your organization's scale, technical infrastructure, and AI readiness before scheduling a discovery call. Not every application moves forward — and that's intentional.
02
Architecture Discovery
A 2-hour working session with the ARX technical team. We map your current operations, identify automation opportunities, and define what "success" means for your organization specifically.
03
Custom Proposal
We deliver a full technical proposal — architecture diagrams, integration plan, compliance framework, timeline, and pricing. You'll know exactly what you're getting before signing anything.
04
Build & Deploy
Our team builds in parallel with yours. Staged deployment with testing gates at each phase. No big-bang launch — controlled rollout with rollback protocols at every stage.

Ongoing Partnership
Post-deployment isn't the end — it's the beginning. Quarterly reviews, continuous model improvement, and a dedicated ARX contact embedded in your operations indefinitely.
